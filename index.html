Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and solutions

What makes Dormache Empire different from other digital agencies?

Dormache Empire stands out through our unique combination of expertise, innovation, and client-centric approach. We don't just build websites or implement tools—we become strategic partners in your growth journey. Our team brings together world-class developers, AI specialists, and business strategists who work collaboratively to deliver solutions that drive real, measurable results. We're committed to transparency, continuous improvement, and going above and beyond to exceed expectations. Plus, our 99.9% client satisfaction rate speaks for itself.

How long does it typically take to see results from your solutions?

Timeline varies based on your specific project and goals, but we typically see measurable improvements within 30-60 days. For custom web development, you'll see your new platform live within 4-12 weeks depending on complexity. AI automation implementations usually show productivity gains within 2-4 weeks. Our average client experiences a 150% ROI within the first year. We provide detailed progress tracking and regular reporting so you can see exactly how your investment is performing.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance after project completion?

Absolutely! We believe the relationship doesn't end at project launch—it's just beginning. We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages ranging from basic updates to full managed services. Our support team is available 24/7 to ensure your systems run smoothly. We provide regular security updates, performance optimization, feature enhancements, and strategic guidance to help you continue maximizing your investment. Most of our clients maintain ongoing partnerships with us to stay ahead of technological changes.

How secure are your cloud solutions and how do you protect our data?

Security is our top priority. Our cloud infrastructure meets and exceeds industry standards including ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and GDPR compliance. We implement military-grade encryption for data in transit and at rest, multi-factor authentication, regular security audits, and automated threat detection. Your data is backed up redundantly across multiple geographic locations with 99.99% uptime guarantee. We conduct regular penetration testing and security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities proactively. You'll have complete visibility into your data and security measures.

What's the investment range for your services, and do you offer flexible pricing options?

Our pricing is flexible and tailored to your budget and requirements. Custom web development projects typically range from $15K to $150K+ depending on complexity and features. AI automation solutions start at $5K monthly for basic implementation and scale up based on usage and complexity. Cloud solutions are priced on a pay-as-you-grow model starting at $500/month. We offer fixed-price projects, monthly retainers, performance-based pricing, and hybrid models. Schedule a consultation with our team to discuss your specific needs and we'll provide a customized proposal that fits your budget.
